Tower T001 - Transmission Tower Specification and Maintenance Report

Location: Tower_001
Coordinates: 28.7041, 77.1025
Transmission Line: Delhi-Gurgaon 220kV Main Line
Tower Height: 45 meters
Foundation Depth: 3.5 meters

STRUCTURAL SPECIFICATIONS:
The tower is constructed with steel lattice structure using high-grade steel angles and channels. The main vertical members are fabricated from 150x150x12mm angles with a yield strength of 250 MPa. The horizontal bracing consists of 100x100x10mm angles providing lateral stability. The tower is designed to withstand wind speeds up to 150 km/hr and temperature variations from -5°C to +50°C.

ELECTRICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
The tower carries three 220kV transmission lines with phase spacing of 4.5 meters. Each phase is suspended using ceramic disk insulators rated at 220kV. The earth wire is suspended at the top using ACSR conductor of 50 sq.mm. The tower is equipped with a grounding system consisting of four ground rods driven 10 meters deep into the earth with a total resistance of 2.5 ohms.
