Tower T002 - Transmission Tower Specification and Maintenance Report

Location: Tower_002
Coordinates: 28.7082, 77.1042
Transmission Line: Delhi-Gurgaon 220kV Main Line
Tower Height: 45 meters
Foundation Depth: 3.5 meters

STRUCTURAL SPECIFICATIONS:
Similar to Tower T001, this tower features a steel lattice structure with 150x150x12mm main vertical members and 100x100x10mm horizontal bracing. The design is identical to other towers on the same transmission line for consistency and ease of maintenance.

ELECTRICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
Carries three 220kV phase conductors with ACSR 240 sq.mm conductor size. The tower is equipped with ceramic disk insulators rated at 220kV, suspended in a vertical string arrangement. The earth wire is ACSR 50 sq.mm. Grounding resistance is 2.3 ohms.

INSPECTION AND MAINTENANCE HISTORY:
Date: 2024-02-10
Last Inspection: Found corrosion on bolted connections at height 15-18 meters. Some paint peeling observed at various locations.
Action Taken: Applied protective coating on corroded areas. Bolts were cleaned and tightened.

Date: 2024-03-05
Vibration Analysis: Conducted vibration measurements during high wind conditions. Frequency response within acceptable limits. No resonance issues detected.

FAULT HISTORY:
2024-01-22: Single phase fault (Phase C) due to insulator degradation. Fault duration: 45ms. Cause: Surface contamination and moisture ingress.
Action: Replaced the affected insulator unit.

RECENT CONDITION:
Tower is in moderate condition. Ongoing corrosion at higher levels requires monitoring. Last major refurbishment was in 2019. Next major maintenance scheduled for Q3 2024.
