STRUCTURAL SPECIFICATIONS:
Standard lattice tower design with 150x150x12mm main members. This tower is located near an industrial complex requiring enhanced monitoring for vibration and electromagnetic interference.

ELECTRICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
Three 220kV phase conductors. The tower carries additional communication cables in the earth wire space. Uses standard ceramic disk insulators. Grounding system consists of four ground rods with total resistance of 2.4 ohms.

INSPECTION AND MAINTENANCE HISTORY:
Date: 2024-03-10
Inspection: Moderate corrosion detected on connection plates. Some bolts showing signs of loosening due to vibration from adjacent industrial machinery.
Action Taken: Bolts retightened, connection plates cleaned and protective coating applied.

Date: 2024-01-25
Vibration Study: High-frequency vibrations detected (12-15 Hz) due to operation of industrial equipment nearby. Frequency response monitoring established.
